Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

Privacy-First Promise: VaultTrak is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We do NOT collect, store, or access your data on external servers. Everything stays on your device, under your control.

1. Our Privacy-First Approach

VaultTrak is built on the following privacy principles:

  • No External Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or access your data on any external servers
  • Local Storage Only: All your data is stored exclusively on your device
  • Optional iCloud Sync: If you choose to enable iCloud sync, data is synced through your personal Apple account (not our servers)
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies
  • No Data Sharing: We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties

2. Data We Store (Locally on Your Device)

VaultTrak stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Career Information: Job applications, employment history, skills, certifications, and career goals
  • Financial Information: Bills, payments, transactions, receipts, and expense tracking
  • Personal Content: Notes, journal entries, voice memos, and photos you add to the app
  • Schedule Information: Interview appointments, reminders, deadlines, and calendar events
  • Profile Information: Your name, contact information, and profile photo (if provided)
  • Time Tracking Data: Time entries, project details, and work logs

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. VaultTrak does not have access to this data on any external servers.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your data is encrypted and stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework, which provides:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest using Apple's security infrastructure
  • Sandboxing: App data is isolated and protected by iOS/macOS security
  • Device Security: Protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID

Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync (optional), your data is:

  • Synced via iCloud: Data syncs through your personal Apple iCloud account
  • Encrypted in Transit: Data is encrypted during transmission to/from iCloud
  • Encrypted at Rest: Data is encrypted while stored in your iCloud account
  • Apple-Managed: iCloud storage and security is managed by Apple, not VaultTrak
  • Your Control: You can disable iCloud sync anytime in app settings
Note: When using iCloud sync, your data is subject to Apple's iCloud Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. VaultTrak does not have access to your iCloud data.

Additional Security Features

  • Face ID / Touch ID: Optional biometric authentication for app access
  • PIN Protection: Optional PIN code for securing the app
  • Auto-Lock: Configurable timeout to automatically lock the app

4. Third-Party Services (Apple Only)

VaultTrak integrates only with Apple's native services and frameworks. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Apple Services We Use:

  • iCloud: Optional cloud sync (requires your permission and iCloud account)
  • StoreKit: For processing subscription purchases through Apple
  • Apple Calendar: Optional integration for importing/exporting events (requires permission)
  • Apple Reminders: Optional integration for task management (requires permission)
  • Apple Notes: Optional integration for note-taking (requires permission)
  • Apple Contacts: Optional access for contact information (requires permission)
  • VisionKit: For scanning receipts and documents using device camera
  • PhotoKit: For accessing photos you choose to add (requires permission)

Important: All these integrations are optional and require your explicit permission. You can revoke permissions anytime in iOS/macOS Settings.

5. What We Do NOT Do

To be completely transparent, here's what VaultTrak does NOT do:

  • ❌ We do NOT collect data on external servers
  • ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • ❌ We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT use advertising networks or show ads
  • ❌ We do NOT access your data remotely
  • ❌ We do NOT use AI services that process your data externally
  • ❌ We do NOT require account creation or login
  • ❌ We do NOT track your location
  • ❌ We do NOT collect usage statistics

6. Your Rights and Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible directly in the app
  • Edit: You can edit or correct any information anytime
  • Delete: You can delete any data or all data instantly in the app
  • Export: Export your data in PDF or CSV format for backup or migration
  • Control Sync: Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time
  • Revoke Permissions: Remove app permissions for Calendar, Photos, etc. in device Settings
  • No Account Required: No login or account creation means nothing to "delete" from our servers

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device as long as you keep the app installed:

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • iCloud Data: If sync is enabled, data remains in your iCloud account according to Apple's policies
  • Complete Deletion: When you delete the app, all local data is removed from your device
  • iCloud Deletion: To delete iCloud data, disable sync and delete the data from iCloud Settings

Important: Since we don't store your data on our servers, there's nothing for us to delete when you stop using the app. Simply delete the app from your device to remove all local data.

8. Permissions and Access

VaultTrak may request the following permissions (all optional):

  • Camera: For scanning receipts and documents
  • Photos: For attaching images to entries
  • Calendar: For syncing interview appointments and deadlines
  • Reminders: For task management integration
  • Contacts: For adding contact information to job applications
  • Notifications: For reminders and alerts

You can grant or revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device Settings. The app functions without these permissions, though some features may be unavailable.

9. Children's Privacy

VaultTrak is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since all data is stored locally on the device, parents should monitor their children's device usage.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in:

  • App features and functionality
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Privacy best practices

Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Legal Compliance

While VaultTrak does not collect data on external servers, we may be required to comply with legal obligations:

  • We may be required to provide information if compelled by law enforcement with proper legal process
  • However, since we don't have access to your data stored locally or in your iCloud, we cannot provide what we don't have
  • Any legal requests would need to be directed to Apple for iCloud data

12. International Users

VaultTrak is available worldwide. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location. If you use iCloud sync, your data is stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure according to Apple's data residency policies.
