Frequently Asked Questions

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Getting Started

Q: What is VaultTrak?
A: VaultTrak is an all-in-one iOS app for tracking your career, finances, and personal productivity. It helps you manage job applications, active jobs, bills, bank accounts, skills, certifications, tasks, and more—all in one secure place.
Q: What devices does VaultTrak work on?
A: VaultTrak works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac (via Catalyst). It requires iOS 17 or later.
Q: Is VaultTrak free?
A: VaultTrak offers a 7-day free trial for all premium features. After the trial, you can subscribe to VaultTrak Pro (monthly or yearly) or continue using limited free features.

Data & Privacy

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted SwiftData framework. We do NOT collect, access, or store your data on external servers. Your data is 100% yours.
Q: Does VaultTrak sync across devices?
A: Yes! You can enable optional iCloud Sync to automatically sync your data across all your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac). iCloud sync is encrypted and managed by Apple—we never access your iCloud data.
Q: How do I enable iCloud Sync?
A: Go to Profile Tab → Settings → iCloud Sync & Data and toggle "Enable iCloud Sync". Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all your devices.
Q: What happens if I disable iCloud Sync?
A: Your data will remain stored locally on each device, but changes won't sync between them. You can re-enable sync anytime—VaultTrak will merge data from all devices.
Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes! You can export your data in PDF or CSV formats from within the app. Go to Profile Tab → Settings → iCloud Sync & Data → Export Data.
Q: What if I delete the app?
A: Deleting the app removes all local data from that device. If you have iCloud Sync enabled, your data remains in your personal iCloud account and can be restored by reinstalling the app and signing back into iCloud.

Security

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes! VaultTrak uses Apple's built-in encryption for local storage. Additionally, you can enable:
  • PIN Code protection (4-digit PIN)
  • Face ID / Touch ID biometric authentication
  • Auto-lock after inactivity (configurable timeout)
Q: How do I set up a PIN?
A: Go to Profile Tab → Settings → Security → Set PIN. Once set, you'll be prompted to unlock the app when you open it or after auto-lock timeout.
Q: How does Face ID / Touch ID work?
A: After setting a PIN, you can enable biometric authentication in Profile Tab → Settings → Security → Enable Face ID/Touch ID. This lets you unlock the app with your face or fingerprint instead of typing your PIN.
Q: What is auto-lock?
A: Auto-lock automatically locks the app after a period of inactivity (5 minutes by default). You can adjust this in Profile Tab → Settings → Security → Auto-Lock Time (options: immediately, 1, 5, 15, 30, 60 minutes, or never).
Q: I forgot my PIN. What do I do?
A: Unfortunately, for security reasons, there is no PIN recovery. You'll need to delete and reinstall the app, which will erase local data. If you had iCloud Sync enabled, your data will be restored after reinstalling.

Subscriptions & Premium Features

Q: What's included in the free trial?
A: The 7-day free trial includes all premium features:
  • Unlimited job applications, jobs, bills, and financial records
  • Resume parser & optimization
  • Advanced analytics & reports
  • Unlimited voice memos
  • Manual bank account tracking
Q: What happens after the trial ends?
A: After 7 days, you'll need to subscribe to VaultTrak Pro (monthly or yearly) to continue using premium features. Some basic features remain free.
Q: What are the subscription prices?
A:
  • Monthly: Check the app for current pricing
  • Yearly: Check the app for current pricing (usually discounted vs monthly)
All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID and can be canceled anytime.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → VaultTrak and tap "Cancel Subscription". You can also manage subscriptions from Profile Tab → Settings → Subscription Management → Manage Subscription.
Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your VaultTrak purchase, and request a refund. Apple reviews all refund requests.

Features

Q: What can I track in the Career tab?
A:
  • Job applications (status pipeline, interview dates, contacts)
  • Active jobs (salary, pay frequency, employment dates)
  • Skills (with proficiency levels)
  • Certifications (with expiration tracking)
  • Courses & training
  • Professional network contacts
  • Career goals & achievements
Q: What can I track in the Finance tab?
A:
  • Bills (due dates, amounts, categories, recurring bills)
  • Income & expenses (manual entry)
  • Bank accounts (balances and transactions - all manually entered)
  • Payment history from jobs
  • Financial statistics (income, expenses, net income by period)
Q: Does VaultTrak connect to my bank?
A: No, VaultTrak does not connect directly to your bank. All bank account information must be entered manually. You can manually track:
  • Account balances
  • Transactions
  • Spending by category
  • Financial summaries
This manual approach ensures your banking credentials stay completely private and secure.
Q: How does the resume parser work?
A: Upload your resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) and VaultTrak will automatically extract:
  • Job history (companies, positions, dates, responsibilities)
  • Skills (with auto-categorization)
  • Certifications
  • Education
You can then review and edit the parsed data before saving it to your profile.
Q: Can I import bank statements?
A: Yes! VaultTrak can parse CSV bank statements from supported formats (currently US Bank). Go to Finance Tab → Bank Accounts → Import Statement.
Q: What notifications can VaultTrak send?
A: VaultTrak can send reminders for:
  • Upcoming interviews
  • Follow-up deadlines for job applications
  • Bill due dates
  • Overdue bills
  • Certification expirations
  • Task deadlines
  • Weekly application goal progress
You can customize notification settings in Profile Tab → Settings → Notifications.

Integrations

Q: Does VaultTrak work with Apple Calendar?
A: Yes! VaultTrak can sync interviews and bill due dates to your Apple Calendar. Enable this in Profile Tab → Settings → Calendar Integration.
Q: Does VaultTrak work with Siri?
A: Yes! VaultTrak supports Siri Shortcuts. You can say:
  • "Hey Siri, show my VaultTrak dashboard"
  • "Hey Siri, view my interviews"
  • "Hey Siri, add a job application"
Set up shortcuts in iOS Settings → Siri & Search → VaultTrak.
Q: Does VaultTrak appear in Spotlight search?
A: Yes! All your jobs, applications, bills, and tasks are indexed in iOS Spotlight. Just search for them from your home screen.
Q: Can I use VaultTrak with Apple Reminders?
A: Yes! VaultTrak can create reminders in your Apple Reminders app for tasks and deadlines.

Troubleshooting

Q: VaultTrak isn't syncing between my devices. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  1. Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices
  2. Check that iCloud Sync is enabled in Profile Tab → Settings → iCloud Sync & Data
  3. Verify you have enough iCloud storage available (check iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage)
  4. Force close VaultTrak on all devices and reopen
  5. Try toggling iCloud Sync off and back on
  6. If problems persist, contact vault@contentvaultmanager.com
Q: My resume upload failed. What should I do?
A: Make sure:
  • Your resume file is in a supported format (PDF, DOCX, or TXT)
  • The file size is under 10MB
  • You have internet connection (for LLM parsing)
  • LLM Resume Optimizer is configured correctly (Profile Tab → Settings → LLM Resume Optimizer)
If you see a 401/403 error, check your API key. If you see a connection error, verify your Base URL.
Q: The app keeps crashing. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  1. Update VaultTrak to the latest version (App Store → Updates)
  2. Update your iPhone to the latest iOS version
  3. Force close VaultTrak and restart your iPhone
  4. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall VaultTrak (make sure iCloud Sync is enabled first to preserve data)
  5. Contact vault@contentvaultmanager.com with details about when the crash occurs
